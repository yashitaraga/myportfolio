Clinical Decision Rule for Glioblastoma (OpenEMR)

Project Overview

Developed a clinical decision rule (CDR) aimed at assisting healthcare providers in managing glioblastoma treatment decisions more effectively. The CDR specifically addresses malignant brain tumors, providing age-specific insights to help clinicians select the most effective treatment pathways.

Key Objectives

  • Design evidence-based clinical decision rules for glioblastoma.
  • Integrate clinical rules into OpenEMR for real-time clinical decision support.
  • Enhance personalized medicine by including demographic and age-specific criteria.

Technologies & Tools Used

  • OpenEMR Clinical Decision Support module
  • HL7 standards (FHIR)
  • SQL databases for patient data integration
  • Python scripting for rule logic and integration

Outcomes

The developed CDR significantly streamlined clinical workflows, improved decision-making efficiency, and contributed to enhanced patient outcomes by tailoring treatments to patient-specific factors.
